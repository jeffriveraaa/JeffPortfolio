About Me

My name is Jefferson S. Rivera and I’m an aspiring web developer, graphic designer, video editor and businessman. I have passion in creating or designing websites and graphic designs. I always see my self enjoying in designing and editing, I may have no work experience as of now but I can say that I have a background in the ambitions that I want to pursue.

I started programming when I was in my junior high school level. It all started when I own an outdated android phone(4.4 Kitkat) and it doesn’t have an Android Lolipop(5.1) update. In that time, I really want to experience the look and feel of the latest android version(Adroid 5.0) and I can’t afford to buy a new phone for it, so what I did is I modified the stock rom of my device and made its UI/UX looks and feels like an Android Lollipop, I edited system parts which are layouts, icons, animations, volume packs, etc in the system as long as I achieve the latest android Lollipop. As my project completed, I didn’t expected that it will blow up and will be used by many users even in other countries, I only released it for my device then some developers/porters ported it to different phones for them to use it. It was reviewed by many authors around XDA Developers, Xiaomi EU, Facebook Community, etc. Since then, I found myself enjoying in designing a system, applications, websites, and graphics.

The kind of design I always do and trying to achieve is clean and user friendly. I cannot say that I’m perfect but when it comes to designing, I’m very strict to myself nor perfectionist as I always wants to achieve the standards of myself and users. When I was in my senior high school level, I started building simple websites but I lost them because my old laptop was broken, but I was able to save some of my school projects in my college years. I can’t tell that I am good at programming, all I can say is I just have an idea and background how to play with it like where to place this code, what’s the function of this code, fixing bugs thru trials and errors, reading source code, understanding the flow. What I’m trying to say is I can’t build a project by creating a source code without the help of internet tutorials, forums, etc, because when I’m making a project, researching is always a big part for me, especially when I want some features, ideas, and help.

When pandemic came, my programming habit was slowly stopped because I became a professional esports player and became my source of income. I have a contract so I am obligated to focus in my gaming career while studying online. I didn’t regret entering this career because by this, I was able to buy things that I only dreamt before such as expensive phones, accessories, etc and thankfully my own car. But the time I finished my college, I needed to let go of this even I may have a better income in this. It came to my realization that I will continue my IT career.

In my final semester in college level, it was our OJT, and I was assigned as a graphics design intern at Pinklestar Inc. It was fun and at the same time learning new things especially in designing. I may have a strict mentors and bosses that criticizing my works especially in my early days, but I took all of it as a lesson for me to become better, and happily I got a perfect grade(1.0) in my OJT and became a dean’s lister for the final semester.

I may be independent when it comes in creating a projects, but I always humbly take helps for those who are willing, also I don’t feel offended when some are criticizing my works especially those who has more experience and higher level than me, what I do is I will take note of what they said and I’ll try to achieve what they want especially if they are my boss.
